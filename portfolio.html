Recent Work

Chestnut Home

The Chestnut Home is an off the grid home that our first to
combat against dirty electricity. Solar panels, skylights and
thermal concrete walls all work together to trap heat and power this home.

Hooper Home

The Hooper Home is a restored farm house that features all
green materials. Given a sleek modern interior, this home also
features and underground tank used to store rain water to help reduce
water costs. Solar panels were also added to the barn to assist with land maintenance.

The Annex

We built a state of the art, fully LEED compliant science annex to our
local state college. The walls are constructed from earth bricks that were made on-site, and the greenhouse features Low-E windows.
